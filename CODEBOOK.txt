This file describes the columns in output.txt.  

The first two columns are the Activity and Subject respectively.  Activity has a descriptive name, subject uses the correct identifier.

Each remaining column represents the mean of one of the original columns in the SAMSUNG data.  The same name is used.  The mean is calculated by Activity and Subject, giving a total of 180 rows of information.  There are a total of 79 mean columns, 1 Activity column (Label) and one Subject Column.
